How Your Premium is Calculated

Step 1: Base Premium ($100) - Everyone starts here

Step 2: Risk Index - Your driving compared to average (1.0 = average)

Step 3: EWMA Smoothed - Risk smoothed over time to prevent wild swings

Step 4: Telematics Factor - Final adjustment (capped for protection)

Final: Base Premium × Telematics Factor = Your Premium

Consumer Protection: Even if your risk is very high (like 2.679), the telematics factor is capped at reasonable levels to prevent unfair pricing.

Risk Index Scale:

  • 0.5-1.0: Better than average driver
  • 1.0-1.5: Average to slightly risky
  • 1.5-2.0: High risk driver
  • 2.0+: Very high risk driver
